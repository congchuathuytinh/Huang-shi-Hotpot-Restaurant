ABOUT US

Established in 2016, Huang-shi Hotpot Restaurant has become a favorite for hotpot lovers. Known for its fresh ingredients and flavorful broths, Huang-shi offers a warm and inviting dining experience, perfect for enjoying a communal meal with family and friends.

Premium Ingredients: Fresh meats, seafood, and vegetables.

Delicious Broths: Variety of flavorful, meticulously prepared broths.

Cozy Atmosphere: Warm, inviting, perfect for gatherings.
